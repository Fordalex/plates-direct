Fitting Kits

Our fitting kits come with everything you need to apply your new plates:

  • 10 Sticky Pads
  • 2x b.ack, 2x yellow and 2x white screws

How to install

Step 1 - Remove old number plates and clean
Sticky pads work best when applied to a freshly clean suface. Make sure you have dryed the suface thoughly before application.
Step 2 - Apply the sticky pads
Using 5 sticky pads per plate, peel the protective backing off the pads and space them evenly across the back of the number plate.
