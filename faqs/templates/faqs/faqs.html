How long can I expect to wait for my nuber plates?

Orders are made and dispatched within 1 working day (usually same day, an email will be supplied once dispatched) shipping usually took 3-5 working days. (royal mail 1st class)

Will you make plates with an illegal spacing?

To mid-space or misrepresent the digits on number plates is an offence under the RoadVehiles (Registration & Licensing) Regulation 1975. Regardless of how you enter the spacing in your order, we will space the digits legally and the plates you order will be despatched with the correct & legal spacing of digits
